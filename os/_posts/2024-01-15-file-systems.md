## Performance Metrics
### Disk Utilization
$$ \text{Disk Utilization} = \frac{\text{Used Space}}{\text{Total Space}} \times 100\% $$

### File Access Time
$$ \text{Access Time} = \text{Seek Time} + \text{Rotational Delay} + \text{Transfer Time} $$
